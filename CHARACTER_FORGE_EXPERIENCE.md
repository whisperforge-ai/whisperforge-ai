# WhisperForge AI - Character Forge Experience

## Goal

Turn character creation from a form into a guided creative conversation.

---

# Current Problem

The character builder feels like paperwork because:

- All fields appear at once
- No examples are provided
- Users do not know where to start

---

# New Experience

The creator is guided step-by-step.

---

## Step 1: The Spark

Start with:

"What is the idea you have for this character?"

WhisperForge helps expand the concept.

---

## Step 2: Identity

Develop:

- Name
- Character type
- Age
- Occupation

---

## Step 3: Personality

Develop:

- Traits
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Motivations

---

## Step 4: Voice

Develop:

- Voice style
- Speaking style
- Emotional tone

---

## Step 5: Story Potential

Generate:

- Background
- Conflicts
- Story hooks
- Future possibilities

---

# Experience Goal

The creator should feel:

"I created someone."

Not:

"I completed a form."
