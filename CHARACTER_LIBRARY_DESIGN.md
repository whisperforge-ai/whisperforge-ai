# WhisperForge AI - Character Library Design

## Current Prototype

Character list shows pre-generated characters before user-created characters.

---

# Problem

A creator's own work should feel important and easy to find.

New creations should not feel hidden.

---

# Improved Structure

## My Characters

Shows:

- User-created characters
- Recently updated characters
- Active projects

---

## Inspiration Library

Shows:

- Sample characters
- Templates
- Examples

Purpose:

Help users get ideas, not replace their creations.

---

# Goal

When a creator returns, they immediately see:

"My creations are here."
