# WhisperForge AI - Demo Flow

## Goal

Show a creator how WhisperForge turns an idea into a finished content concept.

Total Demo Time:

60 seconds

---

# Step 1: Start With An Idea

Creator enters:

"I want a mysterious fantasy audio story."

---

# Step 2: Create Character

WhisperForge generates:

Name:
Viktor Vale

Type:
Mystery Fantasy Character

Personality:
Calm, intelligent, protective

Voice:
Deep, controlled, thoughtful

Backstory:
A centuries-old storyteller with hidden secrets.

---

# Step 3: Generate Episode

Creator chooses:

Scenario:
First Encounter

Mood:
Mysterious

Length:
10 minutes

---

# Step 4: Receive Result

WhisperForge creates:

Episode Title:
The Stranger Beyond Midnight

Includes:

- Scene setup
- Character dialogue
- Performance notes
- Ending hook

---

# Step 5: Continue The Story

Button:

"Create Next Episode"

The creator continues building their story universe.

---

## Demo Goal

The creator should feel:

"I went from an idea to a complete episode concept in minutes."
