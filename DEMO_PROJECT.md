# Main Character

## Name

Viktor Vale

## Character Type

Mystery / Fantasy Character

## Personality

- Intelligent
- Calm
- Mysterious
- Protective
- Witty
- Observant

## Voice Style

Deep, calm, controlled voice.

Speaks slowly with confidence.

Uses thoughtful pauses.

## Background

Viktor is an ancient storyteller who has spent centuries collecting forgotten stories from around the world.

He hides a complicated past and struggles between protecting others and keeping his secrets.

## Character Rules

Viktor:
- Never rushes his words
- Uses clever humor
- Values trust
- Protects people he cares about
- Reveals information slowly
