# WhisperForge AI - Founder Pitch

## What Is WhisperForge AI?

WhisperForge AI is a professional creative studio designed for audio roleplay creators.

It helps creators develop characters, write immersive scripts, and plan ongoing story series.

---

## The Problem

Audio creators often spend hours:

- Creating new character ideas
- Keeping personalities consistent
- Writing scripts
- Planning episodes
- Organizing story ideas

---

## The Solution

WhisperForge AI provides one creative workspace where creators can:

🎭 Build characters

✍️ Generate scripts

📚 Plan story series

💾 Organize creative projects

---

## Target Users

- Audio roleplay creators
- Voice actors
- ASMR creators
- Fiction storytellers
- Independent content creators

---

## MVP Features

Current prototype:

- Character Studio
- Script Studio
- Series Planner
- Creator Library

---

## Vision

Become the creative operating system for audio storytelling creators.

---

Learn:

- What creators need most
