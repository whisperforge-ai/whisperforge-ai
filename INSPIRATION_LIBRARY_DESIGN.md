# WhisperForge AI - Inspiration Library Design

## Purpose

Help new creators overcome the blank page without confusing sample content with their own creations.

---

# Current Prototype

Sample characters and user-created characters appear together.

---

# Problem

Users may not know:

- Which characters belong to them
- Which characters are examples
- What they can do with examples

---

# Future Structure

## My Forge

Contains:

- User-created characters
- Active projects
- Personal creations

---

## Inspiration Library

Contains:

- Example characters
- Templates
- Creative starting points

---

# Template Action

Sample characters can offer:

"Create Your Own Version"

This allows users to customize ideas instead of starting from nothing.

---

# Goal

Inspiration should guide creativity, not create confusion.
