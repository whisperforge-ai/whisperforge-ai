# WhisperForge AI - Product Principles

## Every feature should...

- Reduce creative overwhelm.
- Keep creators in one workspace.
- Eliminate unnecessary searching.
- Guide rather than confuse.
- Turn rough ideas into finished stories.
- Help creators stay consistent.
- Save creators time.
- Encourage creativity without replacing it.

If a feature doesn't help creators create better or faster, it doesn't belong in the MVP.
