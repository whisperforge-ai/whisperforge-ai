# WhisperForge AI - User Simulation Test

## Purpose

Test whether WhisperForge feels valuable and easy to use from different creator perspectives.

---

# User 1: Audio Roleplay Creator

## Situation

A creator makes character-based audio content.

They have ideas but struggle with:

- Keeping characters consistent
- Planning episodes
- Managing story notes

---

## First Experience

Questions:

Does WhisperForge immediately explain its purpose?

Do they know where to start?

---

## First Creation

They create:

- Character
- Voice style
- Personality
- First episode idea

---

## Success Test

They think:

"I can use this every time I create a new story."

---

# User 2: Fantasy Writer

## Situation

A writer has a world idea but needs help organizing it.

---

## First Experience

Questions:

Can they find world-building tools?

Do they feel guided?

---

## First Creation

They create:

- Character
- Location
- Conflict
- Story outline

---

## Success Test

They think:

"This helps me organize my imagination."

---

# User 3: Beginner Creator

## Situation

A person has an idea but has never written a story before.

---

## First Experience

Questions:

Do they feel intimidated?

Do they know what to do?

---

## First Creation

They start with:

"I have an idea..."

WhisperForge helps develop:

- Character
- Setting
- Story direction

---

## Success Test

They think:

"I didn't know how to start, but now I have something."

---

# Overall Test Questions

1. Is the user guided?
2. Is the experience clear?
3. Does the user create something valuable?
4. Would they return?
5. Would they recommend it?
